A distributional reinforcement learning approach using Quantile Regression Deep Q-Networks (QR-DQN) for wildfire suppression. Models the full return distribution instead of expected rewards, enabling risk-aware, stable suppression policies with improved area preservation and reduced burned-cell counts over baseline DQN.
